# MA2 – Relatório Experimental

## 1. Introdução
O cenário experimental utiliza o dataset **140k Real and Fake Face**, composto por 140.000 instâncias de imagens distribuídas igualmente em duas classes: "real" e "fake".  

A divisão dos dados segue o método **holdout**, com:
- Treinamento: 70%
- Validação: 15%
- Teste: 15%

Todas as imagens foram redimensionadas para 256x256 pixels.  

**Palavras do autor do dataset**:  
> "This dataset consists of all 70k REAL faces from the Flickr dataset collected by Nvidia, as well as 70k fake faces sampled from the 1 Million FAKE faces (generated by StyleGAN) that was provided by Bojan. In this dataset, I combined both datasets, resized all images to 256px, and split the data into train, validation, and test sets. CSV files are included for convenience."

> "The dataset contains considerable variation in age, ethnicity, image background, and accessories like eyeglasses, sunglasses, hats, etc. Images were automatically aligned and cropped using dlib. Only images under permissive licenses were collected. Filters and human verification were applied to prune the dataset."

## 5. Teste de Sanidade
Exibição de algumas imagens de cada classe, com suas labels, para verificação visual.  
- Algumas imagens fake podem parecer reais, gerando confusão visual.  
- Objetivo: confirmar a consistência das labels e a qualidade do dataset.
